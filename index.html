About Me

I am a self-taught young entrepreneur, who is committed to providing excellent barbering services. I am also an avid computer science enthusiast who is committed to innovation. I have hardcoded this website myself from scratch and plan on combining web development and automation principles to efficiently carry out barbering services.

Services

Yakas_Cuts offers a variety of services at a very high quality with very affordable prices.

  • Men's Regular Haircut

    Any trimming on the side with a #3 guard or higher, along with a scissor trim on top and line up.

    $15

  • Specialty Haircut

    Any skin fades, tapers, and brooks that use #2 guard or shorter. Scissor trim and lineup included.

    $10
